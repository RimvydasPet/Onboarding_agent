Accountant Role: Responsibilities, Daily Tasks, and KPIs

Purpose
Define the core responsibilities, daily activities, and performance metrics for Accountants to ensure accurate financial reporting, compliance, and operational efficiency.

Scope
Applies to all Accountant roles within the Finance department, including General Accountants, Payroll Accountants, and Staff Accountants.

Core Responsibilities

Financial Record Keeping
Maintain accurate and complete financial records in compliance with GAAP and company policies.
Record daily financial transactions including journal entries, accounts payable, and accounts receivable.
Ensure proper documentation and filing of all financial documents for audit readiness.

Month End and Year End Close
Execute month end closing procedures within established timelines.
Prepare and post monthly adjusting journal entries.
Perform account reconciliations for balance sheet and expense accounts.
Support year end close process and annual audit preparation.

Accounts Payable and Receivable
Process vendor invoices and employee expense reimbursements accurately and timely.
Verify invoices against purchase orders and receipts before payment approval.
Monitor accounts receivable aging and follow up on overdue customer invoices.
Calculate accounts receivable turnover and manage collection activities.

Payroll Processing
Calculate and process payroll for employees including wages, deductions, taxes, and benefits.
Reconcile payroll accounts and verify accuracy of payroll transactions.
Ensure compliance with federal, state, and local payroll tax regulations.
Prepare payroll reports and maintain accurate employee payroll records.
Process overtime earnings, holiday pay, and paid time off deductions.

Bank Reconciliation
Perform daily or weekly bank reconciliations to ensure accuracy of cash accounts.
Investigate and resolve discrepancies between bank statements and general ledger.
Monitor cash flow and prepare cash forecasts for management review.

Financial Reporting
Prepare monthly financial statements including income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement.
Generate variance analysis comparing actual results to budget and prior periods.
Create reports for management decision making including KPI dashboards.
Assist with quarterly and annual financial reporting to stakeholders.

Audit Support
Provide documentation and support for internal and external audits.
Respond to auditor inquiries and address audit findings promptly.
Prepare audit work papers and schedules as requested.
Implement corrective actions based on audit recommendations.

Tax Compliance
Assist with preparation of quarterly tax estimates and annual tax returns.
Ensure accurate calculation and timely filing of sales tax, VAT, and payroll taxes.
Maintain records to support tax filings and respond to tax authority requests.

Policy and Process Improvement
Document and maintain accounting policies and procedures.
Identify opportunities for process automation and efficiency improvements.
Recommend updates to accounting policies based on regulatory changes.
Participate in implementation of new accounting systems or modules.

Daily Tasks

Morning Activities
Review overnight bank transactions and flag unusual activity.
Check email for urgent payment requests or financial inquiries.
Review pending invoice approvals in accounts payable system.
Monitor daily cash position and funding requirements.
