Workplace Safety and Facilities Rules

Purpose: Provide a safe, secure, and functional workplace for all staff and visitors.

Scope: All office locations and company-sponsored events at external venues.

Access and badges
Wear your badge visibly in office areas where required.
Do not allow tailgating through secure doors.
Report lost badges immediately.

Visitors
Visitors must sign in and be escorted unless pre-approved.
Do not share confidential information in public areas (lobbies, elevators).

Safety basics
Keep walkways clear; do not block emergency exits.
Report hazards (spills, damaged cables, broken equipment) promptly.
Only authorized staff may operate restricted equipment.

Emergency procedures
Follow posted evacuation routes.
Participate in drills as required.
In case of emergency, contact local emergency services first, then notify Facilities.

Food and kitchen areas
Label personal food in shared refrigerators.
Clean up after use; dispose of food waste properly.

Incident reporting
Report injuries, near misses, and safety concerns to Facilities and your manager on the same day.
