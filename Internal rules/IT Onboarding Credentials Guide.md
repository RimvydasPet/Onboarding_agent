IT Onboarding Credentials Guide

Overview: This guide explains how new employees receive their company email address and initial password during onboarding.

Company Email Address

How You Get Your Email
Your company email address is automatically created by the IT Help Desk before your first day. The format is typically:
firstname.lastname@techventure.com (default)
firstname.lastinitial@techventure.com (if firstname.lastname is taken)
Custom format (if you requested a specific format during onboarding)

When You Receive It
Your email address will be provided to you:
In your onboarding welcome packet (physical or digital)
By your direct manager on your first day
Via the IT Help Desk if you don't receive it

If You Don't Know Your Email
Contact the IT Help Desk immediately:
Email: helpdesk@techventure.com
Phone: +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX
Slack: #it-support (once you have access)
In-person: IT Help Desk, Building A, Ground Floor

Initial Password

Temporary Password
Your initial password is temporary and will be provided by the IT Help Desk through a secure method:
In person on your first day
Via secure email before your start date
Through your manager if you're remote

Password Requirements
Minimum 12 characters
Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
Cannot contain your username or name
Must be changed on first login

First Login
Use your company email as your username
Enter the temporary password provided by IT
You will be prompted to create a new permanent password
Follow the password policy guidelines

If You Didn't Receive Your Password
Contact the IT Help Desk:
Email: helpdesk@techventure.com
Phone: +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX
They can reset your password or provide a new temporary one

Password Reset & Recovery

Self-Service Password Reset
Once you're logged in, you can reset your password anytime:
Go to the Password Management Portal: https://password.techventure.com
Enter your email and current password
Follow the prompts to create a new password
Changes take effect immediately

If You're Locked Out
After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be locked for 30 minutes
Contact the IT Help Desk to unlock your account immediately
They can also reset your password if needed

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA Setup
All company accounts require MFA for security:
You'll be prompted to set up MFA on first login
Download the Microsoft Authenticator app (recommended) or use SMS
Follow the setup wizard to scan the QR code
Save your backup codes in a safe place

If You Lose Your MFA Device
Contact the IT Help Desk to:
Disable your current MFA method
Set up a new one
Recover access to your account

Email Client Setup

Outlook/Exchange Setup
Your email works with Outlook on desktop and web:
Web: https://outlook.techventure.com
Desktop: Configure with your email and password
Mobile: Use the Outlook app with your credentials

Email Forwarding & Aliases
Contact the IT Help Desk if you need:
Email forwarding to another account
Additional email aliases
Distribution list membership

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Can't remember email format: Contact your manager or IT Help Desk
Temporary password expired: Contact IT Help Desk for a new one
Password reset not working: Try the Password Management Portal or contact IT
Can't access email on phone: Verify MFA is set up; contact IT Help Desk
Account locked after failed logins: Wait 30 minutes or contact IT Help Desk

Contact IT Help Desk

For any credential-related issues:
Email: helpdesk@techventure.com
Phone: +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX
Slack: #it-support
In-person: Building A, Ground Floor
Hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM (local time)

Response time: Usually within 1 hour during business hours

Security Reminders

DO:
Keep your password confidential
Change your password regularly (every 90 days recommended)
Use MFA on all accounts
Report suspicious activity to IT Help Desk immediately

DON'T:
Share your password with anyone, including IT staff
Write your password down or store it unencrypted
Use the same password across multiple systems
Click links in unsolicited emails asking for credentials
