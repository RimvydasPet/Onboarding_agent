# Remote and Hybrid Work Guidelines

## Purpose
Set expectations for remote and hybrid work to support productivity, communication, and well-being.

## Scope
Employees approved for remote or hybrid work arrangements.

## Availability and scheduling
- Maintain core availability windows defined by your team.
- Keep calendar status accurate (focus time, meetings, PTO).
- Respond to urgent messages within a reasonable time during working hours.

## Work environment
- Use a safe, quiet, and ergonomic workspace.
- Protect screens from shoulder-surfing in public locations.
- Use headsets for confidential calls in shared spaces.

## Equipment and expenses
- Company provides a laptop and necessary peripherals as defined by role.
- Internet and utilities are generally the employee’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed.
- Lost or damaged equipment must be reported promptly.

## Security requirements
- Use company VPN when accessing internal systems.
- Do not use personal devices for company data unless explicitly approved.
- Do not print Confidential information at home unless required and securely stored/destroyed.

## Travel while working remotely
Working from a different country requires prior approval due to tax, labor, and security considerations.
