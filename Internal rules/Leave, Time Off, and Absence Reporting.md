Leave, Time Off, and Absence Reporting

Purpose: Provide clear rules for requesting planned leave and reporting unplanned absences.

Scope: All employees.

Types of leave
Paid time off (PTO)
Sick leave
Parental leave
Bereavement leave
Jury duty / civic leave (where applicable)
Unpaid leave (by exception)

Requesting planned leave
Submit PTO requests in the HR system at least 5 business days in advance when possible.
Managers approve based on coverage needs and fairness across the team.
Extended leave may require additional documentation.

Unplanned absences
Notify your manager as early as possible on the day of absence.
Record the absence in the HR system within 24 hours.

Medical documentation
A medical note may be requested if an absence exceeds a defined threshold or if required by local regulation.

Holidays and shutdowns
Company holidays follow the published annual calendar. Some teams may have scheduled maintenance windows; plan leave accordingly.

Escalation
If you cannot resolve leave issues with your manager, contact HR for mediation.
