# Google Workspace Setup Guide

## Overview
TechVenture Solutions uses Google Workspace for email, document collaboration, and team communication. This guide explains how to access and use Google Workspace tools.

## Google Workspace Account

### Your Google Workspace Email
Your company email is hosted on Google Workspace:
- **Email format**: firstname.lastname@techventure.com
- **Access**: https://mail.google.com or https://gmail.techventure.com
- **Username**: Your full email address (firstname.lastname@techventure.com)
- **Password**: Your company password (same as IT credentials)

### First Login
1. Go to https://mail.google.com
2. Enter your company email address
3. Enter your temporary password (provided by IT Help Desk)
4. You will be prompted to change your password
5. Set up 2-Step Verification (MFA) for security
6. Accept Google Workspace terms

## Gmail Setup

### Web Access
- **URL**: https://mail.google.com
- **Mobile**: Download the Gmail app from App Store or Google Play
- **Desktop**: Use Outlook, Thunderbird, or Gmail's native interface

### Desktop Email Client Setup
If you prefer Outlook or another email client:

#### Outlook (Windows/Mac)
1. Open Outlook
2. Add account → Google
3. Sign in with your company email
4. Authorize access when prompted
5. Select sync options (Mail, Calendar, Contacts)
6. Click Done

#### Gmail App (Recommended)
1. Download Gmail from your app store
2. Tap "Add account"
3. Select "Google"
4. Sign in with your company email
5. Allow requested permissions
6. Set up notifications

### Email Settings
- **Signature**: Add your name, title, and contact info in Settings → General
- **Out of Office**: Settings → General → Vacation responder
- **Forwarding**: Settings → Forwarding and POP/IMAP
- **Labels**: Create folders for organizing emails

## Google Drive

### Access Google Drive
- **Web**: https://drive.google.com
- **Mobile**: Download Google Drive app
- **Desktop**: Install Google Drive for Desktop (sync to your computer)

### Your Storage
- **Storage quota**: 100 GB (shared with Gmail and Google Photos)
- **Backup**: Automatically backs up your files
- **Sharing**: Control who can view, comment, or edit your files

### Creating & Sharing Documents
1. Click "New" → Select file type (Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms)
2. Name your document
3. Click "Share" to invite collaborators
4. Set permissions: Viewer, Commenter, or Editor
5. Share via link or email

## Google Docs, Sheets & Slides

### Google Docs (Word Processing)
- **Create**: New → Google Docs
- **Collaborate**: Real-time editing with team members
- **Comments**: Suggest edits or leave comments for review
- **Export**: Download as Word (.docx), PDF, or other formats
- **Templates**: Use built-in templates or create custom ones

### Google Sheets (Spreadsheets)
- **Create**: New → Google Sheets
- **Formulas**: Full Excel-compatible formula support
- **Charts**: Create interactive charts and graphs
- **Pivot Tables**: Analyze data with pivot tables
- **Import**: Import Excel files (.xlsx) directly

### Google Slides (Presentations)
- **Create**: New → Google Slides
- **Templates**: Choose from professional templates
- **Collaborate**: Present while others edit in real-time
- **Speaker Notes**: Add notes visible only to you
- **Export**: Download as PowerPoint (.pptx) or PDF

## Google Calendar

### Access Your Calendar
- **Web**: https://calendar.google.com
- **Mobile**: Download Google Calendar app
- **Outlook**: Sync with Outlook calendar

### Calendar Features
- **Create events**: Click on a time slot or use "Create"
- **Invite attendees**: Add email addresses to send invitations
- **Set reminders**: Get notified before meetings
- **Recurring events**: Set up repeating meetings
- **Time zones**: Automatically adjusts for different time zones

### Sharing Your Calendar
1. Go to Settings → Calendars
2. Select your calendar
3. Under "Share with specific people," click "Add people"
4. Choose permission level: See all event details, See only free/busy, or Make changes
5. Send invitation

## Google Meet (Video Conferencing)

### Starting a Meeting
- **Web**: https://meet.google.com
- **Mobile**: Download Google Meet app
- **Calendar**: Click "Add video conferencing" when creating an event

### Meeting Features
- **Screen sharing**: Share your screen or specific window
- **Recording**: Record meetings (with participant notification)
- **Chat**: Send messages during the call
- **Hand raise**: Indicate you want to speak
- **Captions**: Enable live captions for accessibility

### Meeting Security
- **Waiting room**: Host approves participants before joining
- **Mute controls**: Host can mute all participants
- **Remove participants**: Host can remove disruptive attendees
- **End meeting**: Host can end meeting for all participants

## Mobile Setup

### Android
1. Go to Settings → Accounts
2. Tap "Add account" → Google
3. Sign in with your company email
4. Select apps to sync (Gmail, Calendar, Drive, etc.)
5. Allow permissions

### iPhone/iPad
1. Go to Settings → Mail
2. Tap "Accounts" → "Add Account"
3. Select "Google"
4. Sign in with your company email
5. Allow access to requested data
6. Repeat for Calendar and Contacts if needed

## Security Best Practices

### Protect Your Account
✅ **DO:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable 2-Step Verification immediately
- Review connected apps monthly (Security → Connected apps)
- Use Google's Security Checkup: https://myaccount.google.com/security-checkup
- Log out of shared computers

❌ **DON'T:**
- Share your password with anyone
- Use your work email for personal accounts
- Click suspicious links in emails
- Grant unnecessary app permissions
- Leave your computer unattended while logged in

### Two-Step Verification (2SV)
1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/security
2. Click "2-Step Verification"
3. Follow the setup wizard
4. Choose verification method: Authenticator app (recommended) or SMS
5. Save backup codes in a safe place

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't sign in | Reset password via https://accounts.google.com/signin/recovery or contact IT |
| 2SV locked out | Use backup codes or contact IT Help Desk |
| Can't access Drive | Check storage quota; delete files or contact IT |
| Sync not working | Re-authenticate in Settings or reinstall app |
| Meeting won't start | Check camera/microphone permissions; try different browser |
| Can't share document | Check sharing settings; verify recipient email is correct |
