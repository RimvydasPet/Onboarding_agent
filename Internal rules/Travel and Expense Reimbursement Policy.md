# Travel and Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose
Ensure expenses are necessary, reasonable, properly documented, and compliant with tax rules.

## Scope
All employees incurring business expenses.

## General rules
- Spend company money as if it were your own.
- Choose cost-effective options that meet business needs.
- Obtain pre-approval for travel when required by your team.

## Eligible expenses
- Transportation (economy airfare/rail where practical).
- Lodging at reasonable rates.
- Meals during business travel within guideline limits.
- Business supplies and minor equipment (pre-approved when above threshold).

## Ineligible expenses
- Personal entertainment.
- Alcohol beyond reasonable moderation (subject to local law and manager guidance).
- Fines, penalties, and traffic violations.
- Upgrades and luxury services without documented business justification.

## Receipts and documentation
- Itemized receipts are required for reimbursement above the minimum threshold set in the expense system.
- Missing receipts require a written explanation and may be denied.

## Submission timeline
Submit expenses within 30 days of the expense date unless an exception is approved.

## Approvals and audits
Managers approve expenses; Finance may audit any claim and request additional documentation.

## Fraud and misuse
Misrepresentation or fraudulent claims may lead to disciplinary action, including termination.
