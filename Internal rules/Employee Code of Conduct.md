Employee Code of Conduct

Purpose: Define expected workplace behavior and provide a framework for addressing concerns.

Scope: All employees and contractors, including remote work, business travel, and company events.

Core expectations
Treat others with respect and professionalism.
Do not discriminate or harass anyone.
Act with integrity in all business dealings.
Follow safety and security requirements.

Conflicts of interest
You must disclose potential conflicts (e.g., outside employment, vendor relationships, close relatives in reporting lines). HR and your manager will decide mitigation steps.

Gifts and hospitality
Do not accept gifts that could influence business decisions. Any gift above a nominal value must be reported to HR.

Reporting concerns
You are encouraged to report:
Harassment or discrimination.
Fraud, theft, or misuse of company assets.
Safety hazards and security incidents.
Reports can be made to your manager, HR, or the designated ethics inbox: ethics@example.invalid.

Non-retaliation
Retaliation against someone who raises a concern in good faith is prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

Violations
Violations may result in coaching, written warning, suspension, or termination depending on severity.
